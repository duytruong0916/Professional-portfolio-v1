Expensify
An online expense tracking app.
The application helped users track their income, expenses and offered them a snapshot of their remaining budget to help them avoid overspending

This was my first web application writen in React-Redux environment. Whole web platform (back-end and front-end) was completely done by me. Overall, about 1 month of my full-time work resulted in a responsive web application, with features like:

  • Register/LogIn/LogOut.
  • Add/edit/remove an expense.
  • Search by name/filter by date and amount
